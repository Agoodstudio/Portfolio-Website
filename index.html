About Me

I'm a multifaceted creator with a passion for bringing ideas to life. My world revolves around podcast production, where I wear both the maker and producer hats, crafting engaging audio experiences. Some of the podcasts I've worked on include Packer Principal Podcast, Special Effects Podcast, and Debugged Podcast. In the digital realm, I'm a dedicated Game Developer and Engine Developer, always pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment with my work on the Retro Engine. My skills extend to Flutter Development, building beautiful and functional cross-platform applications. When not coding or producing, I'm often found meticulously editing videos or competing as a proud member and team captain of the Fremont varsity Madden team. Off-screen, I also pursue my creative eye through photography. Beyond my creative pursuits, I'm deeply involved in my community. I dedicated two years to BPA, serving as an officer for one of those years. I also spent a year as the yearbook ad manager, and I've contributed over 100 hours of community service by creating slides for my church. Additionally, I manage the social media for Fremont Esports, and I'm proud to have won the 2023 Congressional App Challenge.
